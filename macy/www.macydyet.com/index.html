Hey there!

My names Macy, its nice to meet you.

I run a freelance practice named salience*. I am a junior designer at EMD Agency.

In 2024 I completed a Bachelor's in Communication Design from AUT, minoring in Creative Entrepreneurship. Throughout my degree, I was able to get a taste for the design community through organising events on the DINZ Student Council.

I love using my creativity and drive to create tangible outcomes, combining my love for art with an interest in the business side of things. Its incredible being able to use my skills in a way that can add value and benefit others lives. I am passionate about showcasing the heart and intent behind a brand through storytelling.  

Keep scrolling to see a snapshot of my work in academic and professional practice.

*Salience means to be a striking point, a feature, to be the top of peoples minds; the fact of being important to or connected with what is happening or being discussed. Providing you the visual property by which your business stands out.
