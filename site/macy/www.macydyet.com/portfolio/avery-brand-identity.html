Visual Identity Design

Within the strategy design for my business class, I developed a Visual Identity. This is for a hypothetical service to be provided by Canvas as an education and integration tool for universities. Therefore my group and I wanted it to stand out from Canvas’s professional branding as a friendly and contemporary AI tool to make the user experience better for students.
